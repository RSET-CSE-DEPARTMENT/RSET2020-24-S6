Soy Downy Mildew

Description: Soy Downy Mildew is a fungal disease that affects soybean plants. It is characterized by yellow or pale green leaves, fuzzy growth on the undersides of leaves, and reduced plant vigor.

Remedy:Apply a fungicide specifically designed for soybean downy mildew control. Ensure proper plant spacing for good air circulation and remove infected plants to prevent the spread of the disease.

Soy Frogeye Leaf Spot

Description: Soy Frogeye Leaf Spot is a fungal disease that affects soybean plants. It causes circular, dark spots with gray centers and reddish-brown borders on the leaves.

Remedy: Apply a fungicide labeled for frogeye leaf spot control. Practice crop rotation and ensure good plant nutrition and balanced soil pH.

Tomato Yellow Leaf Curl Virus

Description: Tomato Yellow Leaf Curl Virus is a viral disease that affects tomato plants. It causes upward curling and yellowing of the leaves, stunted growth, and reduced fruit production.

Remedy:Use resistant tomato varieties, control the whitefly population, and remove infected plants to prevent the spread of the virus.
